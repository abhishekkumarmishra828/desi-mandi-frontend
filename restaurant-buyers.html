About Desi Mandi

Desi Mandi is a direct agriculture marketplace where farmers can sell fresh produce, book tractor transport, compare mandi prices, and connect with verified bulk buyers such as restaurants, hotels, vendors, and local markets.

🌱 Key Features

  • Buy & Sell – Vegetables, fruits, grains, livestock, seeds and farm products.
  • Rent Easily – Tractor, pickup, harvester, rotavator, sprayer and farming tools.
  • Bulk Restaurant Orders – Hotels and restaurants can source fresh products directly from farmers.
  • Local Marketplace – District-wise buyers, mandi rates, and nearby vendors.
  • Multi-language Support – Hindi, English and Bhojpuri for easy farmer access.

How to Buy, Sell & Rent Agricultural Products Online

Farmers, buyers and suppliers can trade agricultural products, rent farm equipment, and grow their agri-business without unnecessary middlemen.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Desi Mandi?

It is a farmer-first platform for selling produce, checking mandi rates, booking equipment, and connecting with verified buyers.

Can restaurants buy directly from farmers?

Yes. Restaurants, hotels, dhabas and cloud kitchens can place bulk requirements and buy fresh produce directly.

Is it useful for farmers?

Yes. Farmers can reduce dependency on middlemen and compare bulk buyers for better price opportunities.

Can I book tractor transport?

Yes. Farmers can book tractor, pickup, harvester and equipment from the equipment booking section.

Which languages are supported?

English, Hindi and Bhojpuri are supported for better local access.
